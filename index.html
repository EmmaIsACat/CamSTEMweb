Who are we?

Emma

Emma has just finished the 3rd year of her physics degree at Cambridge. She studied Maths, Physics, Chemistry and Computer science in first year and then chose to specialise in Physics. She has extensive teaching experience with students ranging from year 6 to year 13, and is able to teach physics, chemistry and maths up to A-level. Emma also has experience of tutoring students for entrance exams (she took 11+, 13+ and 16+ exams herself) and is happy to advise students and parents on the best ways to prepare. Emma has also worked at the Institute of Physics with the teaching team on their project to produce a glossary of terms for A-level physics students. Emma can be contacted by email

Thomas

Thomas has just completed his Masters in Physics at Cambridge and is starting a PhD in Astrophysics in the autumn. Thomas studied Maths, Physics, Computer science and Materials science in first year and then specialised in Physics. Thomas has previously run revision classes for the first year physics students at Fitzwilliam College Cambridge and also has experience tutoring students at A-level and GCSE. Thomas is able to teach Maths and Physics up to first year university level as well as Computer science up to GCSE level. Thomas can be contacted by email
