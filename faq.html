When is tutoring useful?

If you have a good tutor then all tutoring should, in principle, be useful. However there are several factors to consider if you are thinking about hiring a tutor for your child:

  1. Cost - tutoring can be expensive
  2. Time - term time can be busy so it is often better to wait until the school holidays when students have more energy
